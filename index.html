About KENAME

KENAME Sport is a women's football company creating global access to the game through matchday experiences, curated travel, and fan-centered digital storytelling. We connect fans to the world's biggest tournaments, produce matchday storytelling from host cities, and prioritize building pathways for women and girls to experience the beautiful game.
