About KENAME

KENAME Sport is a women's football company creating global access to the game through matchday experiences, curated travel, and fan-centered digital storytelling. We connect fans to the world's biggest tournaments, produce matchday storytelling from host cities, and prioritize building pathways for women and girls to experience the beautiful game.

She Knows Ball Fest · Foundation

Support Our Interns

Help us send the next generation of women's football storytellers to the world's biggest stages. Your contribution funds travel, accreditation, and on-the-ground experiences that open doors for young women in sports media.

Why It Matters

Women's football is growing at an unprecedented pace — but the pipeline of diverse voices telling these stories hasn't kept up. KENAME Sport's intern programme places young women in the press box, behind the camera, and on the ground at marquee tournaments.

Every contribution — big or small — directly covers travel costs, event accreditation, accommodation, and mentorship for our interns. 100% of funds go directly to programme costs.

  • Tournament travel and accommodation costs
  • Press accreditation and media credentials
  • Equipment and production support
  • Mentorship from women's sport media professionals
  • Published bylines and portfolio building
  • Network access and industry connections

Brands and organisations can sponsor a KENAME intern directly — with full co-branding, social recognition, and a dedicated impact report.
